Phase 3: The Turning Point

Stepping Away With A Promise

Early 2023

After 16 years in freight, I made the hardest decision: step away from driving. But I couldn't leave without giving back to the industry that made me who I am.

The Promise: Build the tools we actually need. Create education that's accessible. Protect human decision-making as AI transforms freight. Keep it free for those who need it most.

The Problem: I wasn't a developer. I didn't know distributed systems. I had ideas but no technical foundation to build them. So I made another decision‚Äî teach myself everything from scratch.

Building The Three-App Vision

Designed the complete OpenHWY ecosystem architecture‚Äîthree interconnected apps that work together while maintaining clear boundaries and purposes.
